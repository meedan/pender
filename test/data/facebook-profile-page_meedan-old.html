About
<p><b>Meedan</b> is a non-profit social technology company which aims to increase cross-language interaction on the web, with particular emphasis on translation and aggregation services in <a href="/pages/w/104078649628422">Arabic</a> and <a href="/pages/w/106059522759137">English</a>. Through its use of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_Translation" class="wikipedia">Machine Translation</a> (MT), Machine Augmented Translation (MAT), and distributed human translation, Meedan’s goal is to increase dialogue and exchange between Arabic and English speakers primarily by launching a cross-language forum for conversation and media sharing. This service will be designed to stand as “a digital gathering place for a linguistically, culturally, and geographically diverse community of Arabic and English speaking Internet users”.</p><h2>Background</h2><p>Meedan was founded by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ed_Bice" class="wikipedia">Ed Bice</a> in 2005 and incorporated as a nonprofit charitable organization in 2006. Bice, who was formerly Executive Director of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_People%27s_Opinion_Project" class="wikipedia">The People's Opinion Project</a>, remains CEO with a board comprising <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jon_Corshen" class="wikipedia">Jon Corshen</a>, President of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GoingOn_Technologies" class="wikipedia">GoingOn Technologies</a>, a social media company for <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OnDemand_software" class="wikipedia">onDemand software</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adnan_Hassan" class="wikipedia">Adnan Hassan</a>, Chairman and CEO of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mecasa_Advisors" class="wikipedia">Mecasa Advisors</a> and a former senior adviser and member of the Board of Directors at the <a href="/pages/w/1545495652371747">World Bank</a>, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanan_Heakal" class="wikipedia">Hanan Heakal</a>, Senior Human Resources Manager at <a href="/pages/w/112411102108064">Procter & Gamble</a> in <a href="/pages/w/113576781985833">Cairo</a>, <a href="/pages/w/103984472971800">Egypt</a>.</p>
